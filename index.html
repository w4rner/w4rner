Bio Blurb

W4RNER is how Laurence Warner signs off on his code. He received a M.A. in Computational Social Science from U Chicago in 2019 on a UK Fulbright Postgraduate Award. He was the host of "net:work? A Fulbrighter Conversation" the network's flagship longform content series. He is one of the directors and animators of CrystalCUBED, a Web Design Center who did a recent 22-minute computer generated animation about turning a Flip Phone inside out. He is also one of the authors of DinnerSpinner, developed in collaboration between Fast Feed Apps and U Chicago. His areas of interest include interactive 3D graphics and user interfaces.
